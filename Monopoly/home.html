Welcome to Monopoly!

Hello and thank you for taking interest in my project. I have designed this particular game to take the best from the traditional board game from the box and the digital video games. Here's what I mean...

Board Game

  • There is no system to force the same game with the same rules, so players may play however they like
  • Parts needed to play the game are easy to lose.

Video Game

  • There is a system to force the same game with the same rules, so players may not play however they like
  • Parts needed to play are in the system, so they have better sustainability.

This brings us to the Monopoly game right here on this site. By combining the sustainability of a digital video game and the player empowerment of the board game, welcome to MY Monopoly game!

THIS Game

  • There is a system, but it does not force the same game with the same rules, so players may play however they like
  • Parts needed to play are in the system, so they have better sustainability.

Before you grab a crowd to get a game in, I recommend taking time to understand how to use the functions. There is no automation in this system, which means nothing happens until you tell it to happen. Fortunately for you, I have prepared a guide to help you get started as well as a reference during the use of the system. You can click here or use the navigation menu to access it. You can thank me later. I wish you the best experience with my Monopoly game and feel free to report any issues.
